# FreshTrack - Business Summary

**AI-Powered Food Waste Reduction Platform**

---

## The Problem

### Food Waste is a $1 Trillion Global Problem

- **30%** of all food produced is wasted globally
- UK households waste **£700+** per year on food that goes bad
- Supermarkets lose **£5 billion+** annually from unsold, expired inventory
- Environmental impact: Food waste accounts for **8-10%** of global greenhouse gas emissions

### Current Solutions Fail Consumers

| Problem | Current Apps |
|---------|--------------|
| Users forget what's in their fridge | No smart tracking |
| Food expires before use | No expiry alerts |
| No recipe ideas for ingredients | Generic recipe apps |
| Too much effort to track | Complex logging required |

### Retailers Are Flying Blind

- No visibility into which products expire fastest
- Can't identify high-waste customer segments
- Missing opportunity for targeted promotions
- Competitors (Tesco, Sainsbury's) have no real-time expiry tracking

---

## The Solution

### FreshTrack: Your Fridge's Smart Assistant

**Consumer App:**
- 📸 **Receipt scanning** → Auto-add items with expiry dates
- ⏰ **Expiry tracking** → Smart notifications before food goes bad
- 🍳 **AI recipes** → Generate meals using expiring ingredients
- 🛒 **Shopping list** → Auto-generate from missing recipe ingredients

**B2B Dashboard:**
- 📊 **Waste analytics** → Category breakdowns, weekly trends
- 💡 **AI recommendations** → Actionable insights for retailers
- 👥 **Customer engagement** → Retention metrics, alert performance

## Business Model

### Revenue Streams

#### Consumer (B2C)
- **Freemium model**: Free with ads, Premium at £2.99/month
- Premium features: Unlimited items, no ads, advanced meal planning

#### Enterprise (B2B2C) - Primary Focus
- **White-label partnerships** with supermarkets
- Embed FreshTrack in retailer apps
- Supermarkets pay for:
  - Reduced customer churn
  - Data insights
  - Advertising placement

### Pricing Model

| Tier | Target | Price |

| Consumer Free | Individual users | Free |
